Kreni Villa

Kreni Villa is a serene escape in the heart of Ksamil, just 700 meters from Ksamil Beach 9 and Paradise Beach. We offer comfortable, air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms and balconies. Some units feature fully equipped kitchens for added convenience. Our family-friendly accommodations include wardrobes and cozy seating areas. Guests can unwind in our lush garden, perfect for relaxation after exploring the nearby beaches. Lori Beach is also just a short walk away, less than 1 kilometer from our property.
