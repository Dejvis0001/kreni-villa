Welcome

Discover Us

Vila Ledio is your perfect retreat in the heart of Ksamil, offering a serene escape with modern comforts. Situated just 600 meters from the pristine Ksamil Beach 9, our spacious 107 m² apartments provide stunning sea views, free WiFi, and free on-site parking. Each air-conditioned unit features a private bathroom and a balcony,

allowing guests to immerse themselves in the picturesque surroundings. Designed with families and non-smokers in mind, our vila ensure a comfortable and peaceful stay. With Paradise Beach and Lori Beach just a short stroll away, Vila Ledio serves as the ideal base for exploring the beautiful Albanian Riviera.
