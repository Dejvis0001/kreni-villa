Vila Ledio
Welcome to Vila Ledio, your perfect retreat in the picturesque town of Ksamil. Our spacious 107 m² apartment offers stunning sea and mountain views, free on-site parking, and a private balcony. Enjoy the comfort of air-conditioned, family-friendly, and non-smoking rooms with complimentary high-speed WiFi and a private bathroom. Located just 600 meters from Ksamil Beach 9 and within walking distance of Paradise Beach and Lori Beach, Vila Ledio provides the ideal blend of tranquility and convenience for a memorable stay.

Rooms & Accommodation
Two-Bedroom Apartment
The apartment has 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a balcony with sea views. For added privacy, the accommodation has a private entrance and soundproofing.
